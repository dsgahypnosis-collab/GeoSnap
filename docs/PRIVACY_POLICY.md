# GeoSnap Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

## Introduction

GeoSnap ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Username and profile preferences
- **Specimen Data**: Photos you capture for identification, field notes, and collection data
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through third-party payment processors (Apple/Google)

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates for geotagging specimens (only when you grant permission)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version for optimization
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with features for improving the experience

### Camera and Photo Access
- We access your camera ONLY when you choose to photograph a specimen
- We access your photo library ONLY when you choose to upload existing images
- Photos are processed for AI identification and stored in your personal collection

## How We Use Your Information

1. **AI-Powered Identification**: Your specimen photos are analyzed by our AI to identify rocks, minerals, and gemstones
2. **Geological Mapping**: Location data creates your personal discovery map and provides geological context
3. **Personalization**: Usage patterns help our AI mentor (Strata) provide tailored learning recommendations
4. **Gamification**: Track your progress, achievements, and explorer level
5. **Service Improvement**: Aggregate, anonymized data helps us improve identification accuracy

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- Specimen photos and field notes are associated with your account
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties
- AI processing is performed securely through our encrypted systems

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **OpenAI**: For AI-powered specimen identification (images sent securely)
- **Apple/Google**: For in-app purchases and subscription management
- **MongoDB**: For secure data storage

## Your Rights and Choices

You can:
- **Access**: View all your stored data within the app
- **Delete**: Remove specimens, field notes, or your entire account
- **Export**: Download your collection data (Pro feature)
- **Opt-out**: Disable location tracking in your device settings
- **Withdraw Consent**: Revoke camera/photo permissions anytime

## Children's Privacy

GeoSnap is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. The app is designed as an educational tool for geology enthusiasts of all ages.

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics: May be retained for service improvement

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

## Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:
- **Email**: privacy@geosnap.app
- **In-App**: Settings > Help > Contact Support

## California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what data we collect and request deletion. Contact us to exercise these rights.

## European Users (GDPR)

For EU users, we process data under legitimate interest for service provision. You have rights to access, rectify, erase, and port your data. Our data processing complies with GDPR requirements.
