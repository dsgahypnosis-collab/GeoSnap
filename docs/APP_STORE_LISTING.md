## App Store Description (4000 characters max)

**Discover Earth's Hidden Stories with AI-Powered Geological Intelligence**

GeoSnap transforms your smartphone into a precision scientific instrument, an interactive museum, and an expedition journal—all powered by advanced AI.

**INSTANT AI IDENTIFICATION**
Point. Snap. Discover. Our GPT-4o powered AI analyzes your specimen photos and identifies rocks, minerals, and gemstones with scientific precision. Get detailed breakdowns including:
• Mineral composition & chemical formula
• Mohs hardness & physical properties
• Formation process & geological era
• Collector value & market insights

**YOUR AI MENTOR: STRATA**
Meet Strata, your personal geological expert. Ask any question about geology, get guided through physical tests, and receive personalized learning recommendations based on your discoveries.

**DEEP TIME VISUALIZATION**
Travel through billions of years of Earth history. See when and how your specimen formed, from the Hadean Eon to present day. Understand the tectonic forces that shaped your discovery.

**THE EXPLORER'S PATH**
Embark on a journey from Field Scout to Cosmic Geologist:
• Complete expedition quests like "Echoes of Ancient Seas"
• Earn XP and unlock achievements
• Track your discovery streak
• Master the art of geological identification

**FIELD NOTEBOOK**
Document your expeditions like a professional geologist:
• Geotagged notes with photos
• Tag specimens and locations
• Build your personal geological archive
• Works offline for remote fieldwork

**THE VAULT**
Your personal geological museum:
• Curated specimen collection
• Grid and detailed views
• Filter by rock type, location, or date
• Export your collection (Pro)

• Crystal Mastery: 1000+ mineral database

**PERFECT FOR**
• Amateur geologists and rock hounds
• Students learning earth science
• Teachers and educators
• Hikers and outdoor enthusiasts
• Collectors and lapidary artists
• Anyone curious about the rocks around them
